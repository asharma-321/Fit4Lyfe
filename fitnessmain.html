Core Exercises

1. V-Ups

Start by lying on your back with your arms up by your ears. Then, simultaneously, lift your legs up while you sit up. Your body should make a 'V' shape and you should try to reach for your toes. Come back down and repeat. Do 3 sets, 10 reps each.

2. Crunches

Start by lying on your back with your arms down by your sides. Keep your shoulders and legs off the ground the whole time and make sure your back is flat on the ground (do not let your back arch). Then, simultaneously, sit up and bring your knees to your chest while trying to reach forwards. Come back down and repeat. Do 3 sets, 10 reps each.

3. Hollow Holds

Start by lying on your back with your arms down by your sides or up by your ears. Keep your shoulders and legs off the ground the whole time and make sure your back is flat on the ground (do not let your back arch). Do 3 sets, hold for 30 seconds.

4. Planks

Start by facing down with either your hands or forearms on the ground. Keep your shoulders overtop your hands or elbows. You should be on your toes and keep your body in a straight line (do not let your back arch). Do 3 sets, hold for 30 seconds.

5. Side Planks

Start with one side of your body towards the ground with either your hand or forearm holding you up. Keep your shoulders overtop your hand or elbow. You should be on the side of your body and keep your body in a straight line. Do 3 sets, hold for 30 seconds on each side.

6. Leg Lifts

Start by lying on your back with your arms by your side and palms on the floor. Put your legs up so you're in an 'L' shape. Slowly lower your legs down as far as they can go but do not let them touch the ground. Then bring your legs back up to the top. Your back should be completely flat on the ground the whole time(do not let your back arch). Do 3 sets, 10 reps each.
