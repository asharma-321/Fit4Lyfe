    • Juice Cleanse

    A juice cleanse is a type of detox where people typically consume only vegetable/ fruit juices for a short period of time. However some juice cleanses allow snacking on cleanse foods such as raw vegetables, like carrots or peppers. There are many different juice cleanses availible, some are all homemade with fresh fruits and vegetables, and other can be store bought. During a juice cleanse it is recommended to have a juice every few hours.

    A typical schedule in a juice cleanse includes
  • A fresh cup of warm lemon water when you wake up
  • 8 to 9 a.m.: Juice, usually a vegetable, green juice
  • 10:30 to 11:30 a.m.: Another juice or a cleanse food or smoothie
  • 1 to 2pm.: Another juice or a cleanse food or smoothie
  • 3 to 4 p.m.: Juice such as the beet, carrot, apple juice
  • 5 to 6.: Another juice or a cleanse food or smoothie
  • 6 to 8 p.m.: Smoothie or almond or cashew nut "milk"
