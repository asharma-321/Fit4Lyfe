Meal Prepping for a Healthy Lifestyle

What even is Meal Planning?

Meal Planning is simply just planning and making some or all of your meals for the week ahead of time.This can happen with four simple steps.

1) buy supplies and containers

2) Choose what recipes you want to make

3) Make time for prepping meals and buying ingredients

4) Start the meal prep by preparing the meals

Why Meal Plan

There are many reasons why one should prepare meals ahead of time. The biggest ones is that it saves time, money, and energy. Spending more time on meal prep is linked to better dietary habits like eating more healthier and fresher foods. Another benefit is that meal prepping helps with portion control. This way you decide how much you should eat per meal before hand. It also helps to add more nutrition into your meals and keeps you energized throughout the day. Meal prepping can always be adjusted to fit you and your family's lifestyles.
